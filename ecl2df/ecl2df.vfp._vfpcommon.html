ecl2df.vfp._vfpcommon

Common functionality for vfp module to extract VFPPROD/VFPINJ data from Eclipse deck to extract the VFPPROD/VFPINJ data from an Eclipse (input) deck as Pandas Dataframes

Data can be extracted from a full Eclipse deck or from individual files. Supports output both in csv format as a pandas DataFrame or in pyarrow and pyarrow.table
